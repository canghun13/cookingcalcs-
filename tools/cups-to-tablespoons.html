How to Use This Converter

Enter an amount, choose your starting unit, and the converter shows the equivalent in cups, tablespoons, teaspoons, and milliliters instantly. Works in both directions — convert cups to tablespoons or tablespoons back to cups.

How Many Tablespoons in a Cup?

There are 16 tablespoons in 1 US cup. This is one of the most useful kitchen conversions for scaling recipes when you're missing the right measuring cup. Quarter cup is 4 tablespoons, third cup is 5 tablespoons plus 1 teaspoon, and half cup is 8 tablespoons.

Why ⅓ Cup Is an Odd Number of Tablespoons

Most cup fractions divide evenly into tablespoons — except ⅓ cup. Because 16 tablespoons doesn't divide evenly by 3, one third of a cup equals 5 tablespoons plus 1 teaspoon (5.333 tablespoons). Similarly, ⅔ cup = 10 tablespoons plus 2 teaspoons. This is why recipes sometimes list ⅓ cup rather than trying to express it in tablespoons.

When This Conversion Comes Up Most

The cups-to-tablespoons conversion is most useful in three situations. First, when a recipe calls for a small amount in cups (like ¼ cup of olive oil) and you'd rather grab the tablespoon measure instead. Second, when scaling a recipe down — if you're halving a recipe that calls for ¾ cup of something, knowing that's 12 tablespoons (and half is 6 tablespoons) is faster than looking for the ⅜ cup mark. Third, when your measuring cup is dirty or missing and tablespoon is what you have.

For converting cups to grams for specific ingredients like flour, sugar, or butter, use the Cups to Grams Calculator instead — volume-to-weight depends on the ingredient's density.

Frequently Asked Questions

How many tablespoons is ¼ cup?

¼ cup equals exactly 4 tablespoons. This comes up frequently in recipes that call for a small amount of butter, oil, or liquid. Four tablespoons is also equal to 2 fluid ounces or 60ml.

How many tablespoons is ½ cup?

½ cup equals 8 tablespoons. For butter, ½ cup is also 1 stick of US butter (113g / 4 oz). If a recipe calls for ½ cup of butter and you're measuring by tablespoon, count out 8.

How many tablespoons is ¾ cup?

¾ cup equals 12 tablespoons. Three-quarters of 16 is 12. In teaspoons that's 36 — but at that volume it's more practical to use tablespoons or the ¾ cup measure directly.

How many tablespoons is ⅛ cup?

⅛ cup equals 2 tablespoons exactly. This comes up with spices, extracts, and small amounts of oil or vinegar in dressings and marinades.

Are cups and tablespoons the same in all countries?

No. This converter uses US measurements: 1 US cup = 240ml, 1 US tablespoon = 14.79ml, so 1 cup = 16 tablespoons. Australian tablespoons are 20ml, making 1 cup about 12 Australian tablespoons. UK and Canadian cups are slightly different from US cups. For international recipes, check which country's measurements the recipe uses before converting.
